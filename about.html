About Aasha Jyoti

Empowering lives through food, education & hope.

Who We Are

Aasha Jyoti is a grassroots NGO driven to uplift underprivileged communities in India by ensuring no child is left behind due to hunger, lack of clothing, or education.

Our Mission

To provide nutritious meals, essential clothing, and educational support to the underserved, so they may live with dignity and opportunity.
